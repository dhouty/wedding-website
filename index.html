Our Love Story

  • Becky and Daniel meet in quiz section at the University of Washington. They get to know each other by working on worksheets and building a chess bot. Becky sends Daniel a Snapchat of herself with a blue beard, which Daniel still has saved as her photo on his phone.
    October to December 2015
  • Daniel admits to Becky that he has been meaning to ask her something: if she would like to go out with him. The next day, they have dinner and see the movie The Revenant. Do they recommend seeing a scary movie on a first date? Only if you want an excuse to cuddle.
    January 2016
  • Becky meets Daniel's family for the first time on a trip to the zoo. They take Becky to the tulip festival for the first time. Becky convinces one of her friends that Daniel was born in a tulip.
    March 2016
  • Becky and Daniel finish the school year, and Daniel goes home for the summer. Daniel drives to Seattle every weekend to visit Becky since she did not know how to drive yet.
    June to September 2016
  • Becky joins Daniel and his family for their annual camping trip. Becky thought she had evaded camping for good before meeting Daniel. Daniel's aunt convinces them to leave the camping trip, buy Nintendo 3DSs, and come back to play Animal Crossing with her and her sons.
    September 2016
  • Becky and Daniel meet their nephew for the first time. He quickly becomes one of the loves of their life.
    December 2016
  • Becky and Daniel celebrate their first anniversary with dinner and a movie. Becky starts saving photos of clothes for wedding inspiration.
    January 2017
  • Becky and Daniel meet their niece for the first time. They are terrified when she is only four pounds, but she grows quickly and is full of life.
    May 2017
  • Becky and Daniel graduate from the University of Washington.
    June 2017
  • Becky and Daniel move in together. She starts asking when he would like to adopt a pet.
    July 2017
  • Becky and Daniel dress up for Halloween for the first time as a couple. He is Finn the Human, and she is Princess Bubblegum from Adventure Time, foreshadowing future hair goals.
    October 2017
  • Becky and Daniel buy their first Christmas tree and celebrate Christmas for the first time in their apartment.
    December 2017
  • Becky finally convinces Daniel to adopt a pet. They visit Seattle Area Feline Rescue and find a pair of siblings cuddling in their litter pan. The tortoiseshell climbs all over Becky and Daniel, and the orange tabby is more than happy to sniff them. They name the tortie Natasha after Black Widow and the orange tabby Cap after Captain America.
    July 2018
  • Becky and Daniel go on their first trip to Disneyland together. They meet their hero Captain America, see all of Mickey's friends in their Halloween costumes, and go on the Haunted Mansion ride and the Winnie the Pooh ride every day. Much to her chagrin, Becky is asked if she has a ring when she comes home.
    October 2018
  • Becky and Daniel go to Hawaii for the first time. They visit Iolani Palace, attend a luau, and go snorkeling at Hanauma Bay.
    November 2019
  • Becky and Daniel celebrate their fourth anniversary. Becky starts sending him engagement rings that she likes.
    January 2020
  • Daniel plans a trip to Disneyland during which he intends to propose to Becky. This trip ultimately is canceled due to events in the following month.
    February 2020
  • Daniel is told to start working from home. Becky spends every day playing Animal Crossing: New Horizons. The kitties think their parents staying home every day is the best surprise ever.
    March 2020
  • Becky and Daniel buy their first house. The kitties have more room to play and quickly pick out their favorite windows in the house.
    September 2020
  • Daniel buys an engagement ring.
    November 2020
  • Becky and Daniel's niece and nephew are finally adopted by their forever family. Their new big sister is loving, sassy, and adventurous. Becky and Daniel are proud to be her auntie and uncle too.
    April 2021
  • Becky and Daniel return to Disneyland. Daniel takes Becky to Sleeping Beauty's Castle and admits again that he has been meaning to ask her something: if she would marry him.
    October 2021
  • Becky and Daniel fly to Las Vegas to see the BTS concert for Becky's birthday. They have lunch at BTS's pop-up restaurant, visit the BTS photo gallery, and wait in line for five hours to buy concert merch.
